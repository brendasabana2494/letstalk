Our Story

Every year, thousands of people, especially the youth, fall victim to mental illnesses, because they are unable to open up to the people who are around them; people who are supposed to help them. As a result they bottle up what they are suffering from, until it’s too late and there is yet another case of a successful suicide attempt.

This is why Lets Talk About It was conceived. We decided that the way we can try to curb this problem is to provide an opportunity for people, especially the youth, to gather in one place and share their problem with strangers. It is easier to talk to strangers about problems than it is to open up to someone we know as we fear judgement from them. No judgement means the youth are able to share their issues with others and prevent depression. In turn that means less people end up depressed or suicidal.
